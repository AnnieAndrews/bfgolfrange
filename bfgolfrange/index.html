Facility

Big Foot Golf Range is a state of the art golf experience sitting on 5 acres of land in Walworth, Wisconsin, near the beautiful Geneva Lake. From beginners to the advanced golfer our range features both synthetic turf stalls and a grass hitting area, a grass putting space, and multiple covered hitting mat areas. Our location offers a tranquil, wooded surrounding with a walking path.

Facility Features

- Beer & wine served during clubhouse hours
- Covered & uncovered outdoor stalls
- Targets
- Putting area
- Limited selection of club rentals
- Clubhouse lounge space
